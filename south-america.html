Overview

South America boasts significant water resources, especially in the Amazon Basin. However, droughts, deforestation, and contamination are pressing issues.

Main Water Sources

  • Amazon River: One of the world's largest river systems.
  • Groundwater: Used extensively in agriculture.
  • Rainfall: High seasonal rainfall in rainforest regions.

Key Challenges

  • Deforestation: Loss of rainforests affects rainfall patterns.
  • Pollution: Industrial and mining pollution.
  • Climate Change: Increasing drought frequency.

Conservation Initiatives

Efforts include reforestation, protected areas, and improved water treatment practices.
