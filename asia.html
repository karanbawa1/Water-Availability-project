Water Availability in Asia

Home | North America | South America | Africa | Europe | Oceania

Overview

Over 75% of Asia is water insecure, with countries that are home to more than 90% of the region's population already facing an imminent water crisis.

Main Water Sources

  • Rivers: Ganges, Yangtze, Mekong, and Indus.
  • Monsoon Rains: Critical seasonal rains.
  • Glacial Melt: Water from Himalayan glaciers.

Key Challenges

  • Population Growth: Strain on water resources.
  • Industrial Pollution: Major concern in urban rivers.
  • Climate Change: Glacial melt and altered rainfall patterns.

Conservation Initiatives

Countries are adopting rainwater harvesting, river cleanup projects, and sustainable agriculture practices.
